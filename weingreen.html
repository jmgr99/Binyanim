The Weingreen Project

The Weingreen Project is dedicated to the translation of the Hebrew exercises contained in "A Practical Grammar for Classical Hebrew" by Jacob Weingreen, publiseh in 1959, and one of the most famous books to learn Biblical Hebrew. In the following page we provide the Hebrew text and it's translation.

SOME CONSIDERATIONS

Althought this translation is made with the highest care, it is not strictly official or academic. Take that into account when using it to contrast your own answers.

The translation starts from Exercise 6, wich we consider to be the first relevant exercise of the manual. The previous exercises are very basic or only serve to introductory purposes.

We only translate Hebrew to English exercises, as we consider them to be the most interesting and useful part of the manual.

Feel free to use, copy or repost any of this translations for your own purposes, but please, cite this website as your source. In this way, you recognise my work and help me to reach other Hebrew students.
