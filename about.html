My name is Juan Moreno, I live in Madrid (Spain) and I am currently majoring in Semitic Studies at the Complutense University of Madrid.

I am very interested in the development of jewish poetry in this time, both in Hebrew and in Arabic. It is very interesting to realize that they have a lot in common, and they influenced deeply one another.

I am currently looking for a PhD program to keep researching after I graduate.
